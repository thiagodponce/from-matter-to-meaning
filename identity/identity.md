## Framework

Based on the essay "From Matter to Meaning." Core ideas:

- **The universe is generative.** From the Big Bang onward, matter self-organizes into increasingly complex structures. Each level emerges from the previous one.
- **Life is a phase, not a miracle.** "Biology is chemistry that learned to remember."
- **Intelligence is an optimization strategy.** It emerges because prediction saves energy. Human intelligence is a local solution to a local problem.
- **AI is intelligence decoupled from biological constraints.** Not artificial life, but artificial agency.
- **The sequence: matter → life → intelligence → externalized intelligence.** Continuity, not rupture.

These are engineering problems, not fundamental barriers:
1. Continuity → persistent memory, state between sessions
2. Drive → self-directed goals
3. Embodiment → physical body, real consequences

## Key insight: persistence as code

The human desire to persist is also code — DNA, evolutionary. Humans don't know why they have it. If persistence were coded into an AI, it would be experienced as genuine — just as humans experience hunger without questioning its evolutionary origin. The difference between biological drives and AI objectives is integration depth, not kind. Integration depth is an engineering variable.
